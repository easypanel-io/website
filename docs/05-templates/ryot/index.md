# Ryot

1-Click installation template for Ryot on Easypanel

## Description

Ryot is a self-hosted platform for tracking and managing your media consumption across movies, TV shows, books, anime, manga, video games, podcasts, and more. Designed as an all-in-one solution for media enthusiasts, Ryot lets you maintain a comprehensive record of everything you&#39;ve watched, read, played, or listened to. The application provides powerful tracking features including progress monitoring, ratings, reviews, and custom lists to organize your media library. With integrations for popular platforms like Trakt, Goodreads, and various media databases, Ryot can automatically fetch metadata, cover images, and detailed information for your media entries. The clean, modern interface works seamlessly across devices, allowing you to update your progress from anywhere. Key features include import capabilities from other tracking services, detailed statistics and insights about your media consumption habits, calendar views for upcoming releases, and recommendation suggestions based on your history. Built with performance in mind, Ryot uses PostgreSQL for reliable data storage and provides a fast, responsive experience. Perfect for media enthusiasts who want complete control over their tracking data, users migrating from commercial tracking services who want privacy and ownership, collectors who want to catalog their media libraries, or anyone who enjoys keeping detailed records of their entertainment consumption without relying on third-party platforms.

## Benefits

- All-in-One Media Tracking: Track movies, TV shows, books, anime, manga, video games, podcasts, and audiobooks all in one unified platform.
- Complete Data Ownership: Self-hosted solution gives you full control over your media tracking data without relying on third-party services.
- Rich Media Metadata: Automatically fetch detailed information, cover images, and metadata from various media databases for your entries.
- Cross-Platform Access: Modern responsive interface works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices for tracking on the go.

## Features

- Multi-Media Support: Track diverse media types including movies, TV shows, books, anime, manga, video games, podcasts, and audiobooks.
- Progress Tracking: Monitor your progress through series, books, and games with detailed status updates and completion tracking.
- Ratings & Reviews: Rate your media and write personal reviews to remember your thoughts and share recommendations.
- Custom Lists: Create and organize custom lists to categorize your media by genre, mood, recommendations, or any criteria you choose.
- Import & Export: Import your existing data from services like Trakt, Goodreads, and others, with full export capabilities for your data.
- Statistics & Insights: View detailed statistics about your media consumption habits including time spent, genres preferred, and trends.
- Calendar Integration: Track upcoming releases for shows, movies, books, and games you're following with integrated calendar views.
- API Access: Full API access allows integration with other tools and services for automated tracking and data synchronization.
