# Yamtrack

1-Click installation template for Yamtrack on Easypanel

## Description

Yamtrack is a self-hosted media tracking application designed to help you keep track of your movies, TV shows, books, video games, and other media consumption. It provides a clean, modern interface for logging what you&#39;re watching, reading, or playing, along with ratings, reviews, and personal notes. Yamtrack features automatic metadata fetching from popular databases, progress tracking, viewing statistics, and customizable lists. The application supports multiple media types and offers detailed analytics about your consumption habits. Built with a focus on privacy and simplicity, Yamtrack keeps all your tracking data under your control. Perfect for media enthusiasts who want to maintain a comprehensive log of their entertainment without relying on third-party services.

## Benefits

- Comprehensive Media Tracking: Track movies, TV shows, books, video games, and more in one unified platform. Keep detailed logs of everything you consume with ratings and reviews.
- Privacy-Focused: Your tracking data stays on your server. No third-party services, no data mining, and complete control over your entertainment history.
- Statistics & Analytics: Gain insights into your media consumption habits with detailed statistics, charts, and analytics about what you watch, read, and play.

## Features

- Multi-Media Support: Track various types of media including movies, TV shows, books, video games, and more, all from a single interface.
- Automatic Metadata: Automatically fetch metadata, posters, descriptions, and information from popular databases to enrich your tracking logs.
- Progress Tracking: Monitor your progress through TV series, book series, and multi-part content with automatic progress calculation.
- Ratings & Reviews: Rate and review everything you consume. Add personal notes, thoughts, and detailed reviews to your tracked items.
- Custom Lists: Create custom lists and collections to organize your media. Build watchlists, reading lists, and curated collections.
- Viewing Statistics: View detailed statistics about your consumption patterns, favorite genres, time spent, and completion rates.
